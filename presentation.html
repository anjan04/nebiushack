How It Works

Combines Karpathy's autoresearch pattern with SONIC's motion tracking rewards. An LLM (DeepSeek-V3 on Nebius) iteratively optimizes 14 DeepMimic reward parameters. Each experiment trains for 3 minutes on a Nebius H200 GPU, evaluates, and commits to git.

Results

55.2 → 82.6 tracking score
3 improvements across 5 experiments, fully autonomous
Key insight: LLM discovered that loosening tracking temperatures (rpos_w_exp 80→50) dramatically improves walking — a 50% gain over baseline
